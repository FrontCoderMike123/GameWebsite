What is it?

Castle Raiders is a 3D Side Scrolling game. This game is set in the Medieval Era. Evil skeletons have taken over your beloved castle. You must reclaim what is yours! There will be 3 levels to battle through, all having their own set of difficulties. Watch out for spikes! Players will be able to navigate through the castle with ease and will be equipped with LEGENDARY weapons to strikemthe enemies. Some things you can expect to encounter are health, extra life, strength and speed potions, traps, spikes, enemies and plenty of moving platforms.
